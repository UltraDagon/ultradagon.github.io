Tetris

How to play:

Click the "Run example" button at the top right of the game window, you may need to refresh the page if nothing happens.

Controls:

Directional arrows/ASD - Move left, right, and down
Up arrow/W/Q - Rotate piece counter clockwise
E - Rotate piece clockwise
Left Shift - Hold piece
R - Restart
G - Toggle guidelines
L - Increase level
